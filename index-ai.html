About the scale

Real scale (default) places every orbit at its true distance in AU — so Amunet hugs the star and Horus sits far out at 46 AU; zoom out to find Shu and Horus. Compressed (toggle in the bottom bar) squeezes the huge 0.05–46 AU range so the whole system fits on screen at once. Body sizes are exaggerated in both modes (true sizes would be invisible). Real figures are in each world's data panel; orbital speeds follow Kepler's laws.
